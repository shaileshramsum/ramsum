About Follow-Up India

We help businesses expand their outreach by providing trained local representatives to deliver information and collect leads. Whether you're in Delhi or Mumbai and want to reach a city in Bihar or anywhere else in India, we make it easy for you.

Our team consists of educated professionals, each equipped to represent your business, convey your message, and gather valuable leads, allowing you to focus on closing deals. For every successful visit, we charge as low as ₹100 per visit depending on the city and service complexity.

Our Services

Local Representation

We provide local, educated representatives who visit businesses on your behalf. Our representatives wear company badges and professional attire, ensuring that your brand is presented with trust and professionalism.

Lead Generation

Our representatives not only present your products or services but also collect contact details and leads, making it easier for your team to follow up and close the deal.

Follow-Up Visits

Our representatives can schedule follow-up visits, ensuring continued engagement with potential clients and gathering more in-depth information on their needs.

Report Delivery

After every visit, you will receive a detailed report outlining the visit summary, leads generated, and feedback, so you know exactly how your outreach is performing.
